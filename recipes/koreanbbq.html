Korean BBQ Ribs

Some delicious korean bbq ribs to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Short ribs
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  1. Tak the membrane off your ribs
  2. Sautee the onions and garlic
  3. Combine the brown sugar and soy sauce
  4. Put the sauteed onions and garlic on your ribs
  5. Top with brown sugar sauce
  6. Cover ribs in foil and cook in the oven at 300 degrees for 2 hours.
